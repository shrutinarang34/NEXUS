## **Why You'll Love Nexus**

Stop wondering where your money goes. Our app gives you the tools to gain clarity, build healthy financial habits, and save more for what truly matters.

### **Key Features**

**1. See Your Full Financial Picture at a Glance**

Our intuitive dashboard brings everything together in one place. Instantly see your spending trends, account balances, and cash flow without getting lost in spreadsheets.

- **Benefit:** Make informed decisions quickly with a clear, visual overview of your financial health.
- **Screenshot to Use:** Take a full-screen shot of the main **Dashboard** page, showing both the "Expenses" and "Accounts" tabs to highlight the comprehensive view. Make sure it's populated with sample data.

**2. Create Budgets That Actually Work**

Our flexible budgeting tool helps you plan your spending and stick to it. Set overall budgets for any period (monthly, yearly, or custom) and allocate specific amounts to categories like groceries, dining out, and shopping.

- **Benefit:** Proactively manage your money, prevent overspending, and reach your savings goals faster.
- **Screenshot to Use:** Capture the **Budget Detail Page** (`/budgets/[id]`). This is your most powerful marketing screenshot. It should showcase the overall progress bar, the category breakdown charts, and the spending trend line.

**3. Automate Your Financial Life**

Set up recurring expenses like rent, subscriptions, or insurance payments just once. Our app will automatically log them for you when they're due, so your records are always accurate with zero effort.

- **Benefit:** Save time and never forget to account for a recurring bill again.
- **Screenshot to Use:** Take a screenshot of the **Recurring Expenses Page** (`/recurring`), showing a list of sample recurring items like "Netflix," "Car Insurance," and "Rent."

**4. Get Smarter with AI-Powered Insights**

Go beyond simple numbers. Our AI analyst studies your habits and provides personalized insights. It will tell you if you're spending more than last month, congratulate you on saving, and highlight trends you might have missed.

- **Benefit:** Understand the story behind your data and get actionable advice to improve your financial habits.
- **Screenshot to Use:** On the main **Dashboard**, capture a close-up of the **Financial Insights** card at the top of either tab. The yellow lightbulb icon and the helpful text are very compelling.

**5. Detailed Tracking for Every Transaction**

Log expenses, deposits, and transfers with ease. Our clean interface allows you to categorize everything, assign payments to specific accounts, and see a complete history of every transaction.

- **Benefit:** Maintain a crystal-clear, searchable record of all your financial activities.
- **Screenshot to Use:** A screenshot of the **Transactions Page** (`/transactions`) showing the detailed list with different transaction types (deposit, expense, transfer) would work well.
